Astros en Movimiento

Astros en Movimiento (Stars in Motion) is a compilation of short fantasy story tales written between 2014 and 2015, and published on 2019. It consists of love, unlove, depress and adventure stories.

The Wisdom of the Moon

The Wisdome of the Moon is the continuation of the book Stars in Motion, with a similar theme as its predecessor, this story tales were written between 2015 and 2016, with a remarkable obscure feeling.

Las Cartas Perdidas

Las Cartas Perdidas (The lost Letters) is a compilation of short story tales written on 2017 with a different feeling and dynamic compared to the two previous books. The medieval and fantasy tales were put aside in order to write more contemporary love tales.

Las Crónicas de Sempiterno: La Caida del Imperio

Las Crónicas de Sempiterno: La Caida del Imperio (The Chronicles of Sempiterno: The Fall of the Empire) is a futuristic and Ucronic novel that explores how Mexico becomes and Empire and how and why it falls.

The different stories can tell a full storie, the storie of The Red Book, which tell stories about love, obsession, passion and fear.
